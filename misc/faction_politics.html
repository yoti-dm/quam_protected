Faction Politics

Relationships between factions

Relationship matrix

Once you have decided on factions in your word, the time comes to decide on relationships between them. Players should likely be considered another faction by themselves as they will grow into a powerful force in any world through the progress of campaign.

The way you can go about this is to create a table with factions for columns and rows, then fill out everything but main diagonal that can be blacked out. Consider that relationship between factions is not neccessarily symetrical, especially where factions are wastly different in size and power. There could be smaller faction almost fully integrated into bigger one considering bigger one its main ally, while the bigger one would only be considered favourable towards smaller one. As a general rule relationships of bigger factions gravitate towards center compared to those of smaller factions.

Relationship level between factions

LevelDescription
-5Faction is considered enemy number 1. Bases are being burned, members are hunted and killed.
-4There is active war between two nations, members are killed on sight.
-3There is active spying and sabotaging of another faction. Scirmishes occour.
-2Membmers of faction are actively hateful towards another faction. Members of both factions are either expelled or forced to renounce competing faction.
-1Members of faction are untrusting of another faction but there is no active animosity.
0Factions have basically no contact
1Members of faction look favorably on members of another faction but there is no active cooperation between the two.
2Needs and goals of another faction are considered when making plans, there is active work for good relationship between factions.
3There are official channels of communication and cooperation between two factions. Any requests for help is seariously considered.
4Factions work together closly with many members having both memberships. There are joint missions and projects combined with fluent exchange of information.
5Factions work together with many of their goals aligned. Some members of higher leadership are also members of another faction.

Updating the table

Once the table is created it should be regulary updated, both as response to player actions and as response to events in the world. Common enemies and goals can bring factions together, scarce resources can drive them appart. This changes can make world seem alive, organic and interesting.
